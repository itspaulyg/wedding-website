Our Engagement Story

We both enjoy riding bikes and so we planned a bike ride and picnic down by the lake on a beautiful August morning. We took our usual route, heading east on 75th street. Once we hit 1st Ave we took a right towards the beach house. We didn't go far before setting up for our picnic. Leah set up the picnic blanket while Paul got the food out, along with the ring! Paul dropped to one knee and proposed right before the lake. Leah gratefully accepted and now we are getting married this summer!
